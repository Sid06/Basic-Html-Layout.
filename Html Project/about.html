ABOUT US

Organisation started by Sidharth,Prakhar and Kuldeep

Blood Donation is helpline which provide blood to the patient across India.It also allows its user to discuss his/her problem with the registered doctors which help to build a bond b/w patient and doctors.It is free and started with a hope that no one across the India die due to lack of blood during the operation. Till now we have: 15089 user registered 4675 doctors registered 114803 blood unit donated 98 Donors Consented to Donate Blood in the last 30 Days 27 Donors Refused to donate Blood in the last 30 Days 6787 Donors did not confirm their willingness or refusal to donate
